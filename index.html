Work Process

Step 1: Obtain Requirements

The first step I take when starting a project is to clearly outline the project parameters. If completing a personal project, I ask myself what I want to see in the end product, why I want to see it, and how I will get there. For client projects, I first ask the clients what they want to see in the end product and why they want to see it. This allows me to decide how I can make the project successful for them.
